Welcome to Student Gyan Movement

Today, 1000 children in Gwalior are out of school – surrounded by poverty, illness and despair; they are fighting a daily battle for their survival. Together, we can help a child and bring hope in their lives. Together, we can bring change and make it last.

What We Offer

India is a country with more than one billion people, and just one-third of them can read. The rapidly growing size of the population, shortages of teachers, books, and basic facilities, and insufficient public funds to cover education costs are some of the nation’s toughest challenges. This is where Children in India are facing basic challenges. According to a study, more than 30% of educational funds are allocated towards higher education, leaving the primary education in India in sway.
SGM is one of the most efficient clubs working in IIITM Gwalior in Madhya Pradesh directly benefitting over 150 children and their families every year, with live welfare projects on education and livelihood. Here is what we offer :

Safety First

We are concerned about safety of our children. Our managers pick them up and drop at gate no. 3 everyday.

Regular Classes

Classes are held regularly from 5:30 pm to 7:00 pm.

Teachers

We have separate teachers for every class and every subject who are devoted towards students.

Sufficient Classrooms

We have separate classroom for every class.

Creative Lessons

Apart from academics, we teach dance, drawing, computer, etc.

Sports Facilities

We are working to introduce sports facilities in SGM.
